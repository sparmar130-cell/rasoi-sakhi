Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel or modify my order?

Since we prepare and cut your vegetables fresh to order, please let us know as soon as possible if you need to cancel or modify your order. You can do this by replying to your order confirmation chat on WhatsApp, or by calling us directly at +91 90991 13823.

How do you ensure the freshness of the vegetables?

We procure our vegetables directly from local farm networks in Gujarat every morning. They are processed in temperature-controlled rooms, vacuum-sealed, and delivered immediately in insulated bags. They stay fresh for 3 to 4 days when kept refrigerated.

Do I need to wash the vegetables again before cooking?

No, there is absolutely no need to wash them. Our vegetables undergo bubble-washing and UV-C sanitization to eliminate dirt, microbes, and pesticide residues. You can dump them directly from the packet into your cooking pan.

How do I order and pay?

Simply browse our menu, add your desired vegetable packs to the cart, fill out your delivery slot, and checkout. Your order details will be saved to our system and you will be redirected to WhatsApp to confirm your slot. You can pay via cash on delivery (COD) or UPI.

What are your delivery locations and charges?

We currently deliver to all residential and commercial addresses across Bharuch, Gujarat. Delivery is free for all orders above ₹299. For orders below ₹299, a flat delivery fee of ₹30 is applied.

Our Story

The Rasoi Sakhi Mission

Rasoi Sakhi is not a vegetable shop. We are a food-tech startup dedicated to reimagining the modern kitchen workflow.

Born in Bharuch, Rasoi Sakhi was created to solve a daily frustration shared by working professionals, homemakers, and busy families: the time and energy spent peeling, washing, and chopping vegetables after a long day.

We believe cooking should be therapeutic, not a chore. By delivering raw ingredients that are already 100% prepared, sanitized, and vacuum-packaged under clinical conditions, we eliminate prep-work. You save up to 45 minutes every single day, cook better, and minimize waste.

Our Pillars

  • Food Safety First: Complete sanitization using modern bubble-washing and chemical-free UV-C treatment tunnels.
  • Zero Waste: You only pay for what goes in your pan. No skins, roots, or spoiled portions.
  • Zero Chemicals: No preservatives, chlorine washes, or coloring agents. Just raw, clean vegetables.
  • Hyper-Local Delivery: Sourced, cleaned, and dispatched locally in Bharuch to ensure absolute freshness.
No Compromise

Clinical Hygiene & Quality

In a post-pandemic world, how your food is handled is as important as the food itself. We run our processing unit like an operating room.

Bubble Washing

Our custom bubble-wash wash chambers use clean water currents and turbulence to extract dirt particles lodged in leafy greens and tubers. This is gentler than hand scrub washing yet 5x more effective.

UV Sanitization

Every cut vegetable travels on a food-grade conveyor through our high-intensity UV-C tunnel. The UV light dismantles the DNA of bacteria, viruses, and molds, providing a 99.9% clean product without adding any chemicals or altering the vegetable's natural flavour.

Hygienic Dehydration

Leftover water is the main cause of vegetable decay. We run processed vegetables through micro-porous centrifugal dewatering drums to dry the outer surface while retaining internal moisture.

Precision Chopping

Our technicians work under full PPE (suits, hairnets, masks, gloves). We use sanitized industrial blades that slice clean without bruising, ensuring maximum shelf-life and nutrient retention.

Hygienic Delivery

Once packed, your vegetables are maintained under strict temperature controls until the final delivery agent hand-delivers them to you in Bharuch.

Setup Guide: Google Sheets Automatic Sync

To sync your orders with Google Sheets in real-time, follow these simple steps:

  1. Open a new Google Sheet.
  2. Go to Extensions > Apps Script.
  3. Delete the default code and paste the following script: 
    function doPost(e) {
  var sheet = SpreadsheetApp.getActiveSpreadsheet().getActiveSheet();
  var data = JSON.parse(e.postData.contents);
  
  var orderId = data.orderId;
  var rows = sheet.getDataRange().getValues();
  var foundRowIndex = -1;
  
  // Search for the Order ID in the first column
  for (var i = 1; i < rows.length; i++) {
    if (String(rows[i][0]).trim() === String(orderId).trim()) {
      foundRowIndex = i + 1;
      break;
    }
  }
  
  var rowData = [
    data.orderId,
    data.date,
    data.customerName,
    data.phone,
    data.email,
    data.address,
    data.landmark,
    data.items,
    data.subtotal,
    data.deliveryCharge,
    data.totalAmount,
    data.paymentMethod,
    data.deliverySlot,
    data.status
  ];
  
  if (foundRowIndex !== -1) {
    sheet.getRange(foundRowIndex, 1, 1, rowData.length).setValues([rowData]);
  } else {
    sheet.appendRow(rowData);
  }
  
  return ContentService.createTextOutput(JSON.stringify({result: "success"})).setMimeType(ContentService.MimeType.JSON);
}
  4. Click Deploy > New Deployment. Select type Web App. Set "Execute as" to "Me" and "Who has access" to "Anyone".
  5. Deploy, copy the Web App URL, and paste it into the setting field above.
